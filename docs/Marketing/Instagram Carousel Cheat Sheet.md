# Instagram Carousel Cheat Sheet

> Use this when building carousel posts for Bloggo's company page or the founder's personal brand.
> Always read `bloggo-brand.md` before writing copy. Tone violations make content unusable.

---

## The Viral Carousel Formula

```
Attention  →  Interest  →  Engagement  →  Aha Moment  →  Action
  Hook         Transition     Tease slides    Climax         CTA
```

---

## Slide-by-Slide Breakdown

### Slide 1 — The Hook (Attention)

**Goal:** Stop the scroll.

**Rules:**
- 3–8 words maximum
- Create curiosity or tension
- Use strong visuals or bold typography

**Hook Types:**

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Weird | "Content is weird now" |
| Contrarian | "Stop posting like this" |
| Curiosity | "Why your posts get ignored" |
| Fear | "This kills your reach" |
| Story | "I almost deleted this app" |
| WTF | "Blogging isn't dead. Writing is." |

**Visual Hook Ideas:**
- Strange or unexpected object
- Bold colors + high contrast
- Big single word or number
- Surprising comparison

---

### Slide 2 — The Transition (Interest)

**Goal:** Explain why the reader should keep swiping.

Answer ONE of these:

| Question | Example |
|----------|---------|
| What will they gain? | "Here's how to fix it" |
| What will they avoid? | "Most creators make this mistake" |
| Why should they trust you? | "I've tested 2000+ posts" |

**Bloggo versions:**
- "Here's what's actually in your camera roll"
- "Most people do this after every trip. It doesn't work."
- "I built a travel app. Here's what I learned about travel memories."

---

### Slides 3–7 — The Tease (Engagement)

**Goal:** Keep people swiping. Reveal information bit by bit.

**Strategy:** Never reveal everything early. Each slide creates curiosity for the next.

**Formats that work:**

- Mistake #1, Mistake #2, Mistake #3… (then: what works instead)
- Step 1, Step 2, Step 3…
- Stat, Stat, Stat… (then: what it means)
- Problem, Problem, Problem… (then: the fix)

**Example structure for Bloggo:**
```
Slide 3 — Problem: You take 500 photos on every trip.
Slide 4 — Problem: You write zero words about any of them.
Slide 5 — Problem: 6 months later, you can't remember the restaurant name.
Slide 6 — Shift: Your photos already have the story inside them.
Slide 7 — Insight: Time + GPS = a complete timeline. Bloggo reads it.
```

---

### Slides 8–9 — The Climax (Aha Moment)

**Goal:** Deliver the big insight. This is the moment people save the post.

**Content types:**
- A framework or system
- A counterintuitive lesson
- A visual comparison or chart
- A step-by-step breakdown
- A single powerful reframe

**Examples for Bloggo:**
- "Your camera roll is already a travel journal. You just can't read it yet."
- "The problem isn't that you don't write. It's that you start from a blank page."
- "On-device AI means your photos never leave your phone. That's privacy by design."

---

### Slide 10 — The Action (CTA)

**Goal:** Tell people what to do next. One action only.

| CTA Type | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Comment | "Comment 'BLOG' and I'll send you early access" |
| Save | "Save this before your next trip" |
| Follow | "Follow for more travel + builder content" |
| Beta / Launch | "Join the Bloggo beta — link in bio" |

**Bloggo-specific CTAs:**
- "Join the Bloggo beta. Link in bio."
- "Save this for your next travel post."
- "Your camera roll is waiting. Link in bio."

---

## Hook Type Reference

| Hook | When to Use | Bloggo Example |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **WTF Hook** | Pattern interrupt, stops scroll cold | "Blogging isn't dead. Writing is." |
| **Story Hook** | Humans love narrative, builds connection | "I found a cat and accidentally built a travel app." |
| **Visual Hook** | Works when image does heavy lifting | Chaotic camera roll → clean blog |
| **Curiosity Hook** | When you have a counterintuitive angle | "Your trip is already written. You just haven't found it." |
| **Contrarian Hook** | Challenges common behavior | "Stop making photo albums. Do this instead." |
| **Fear Hook** | High urgency, use sparingly | "Your travel memories are fading. Here's why." |

---

## Visual Design Rules

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Text per slide | Max 10 words |
| Slide count | 7–10 slides ideal |
| Typography | Big headline, small supporting text |
| Colors | 1 primary + 1 accent (Bloggo: dark navy + white) |
| Whitespace | Minimal clutter — let it breathe |
| Font weight | Bold for hooks, regular for body |

## Pre-Publish Checklist

- [ ] Slide 1 hook is under 8 words
- [ ] Slide 2 answers "why should I care?"
- [ ] Middle slides reveal one idea at a time
- [ ] Slides 8–9 deliver a genuinely useful insight
- [ ] CTA is one clear action
- [ ] Max 10 words per slide
- [ ] Bloggo brand voice check passed
- [ ] No cloud features mentioned (V1 only)
- [ ] "Bloggo" capitalized throughout
