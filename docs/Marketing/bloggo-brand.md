# Bloggo — Brand Guide

> Reference this before writing any copy, ads, social posts, App Store descriptions, or marketing materials.

---

## Brand Identity

**App Name:** Bloggo
**Tagline (working):** "Your trips, turned into stories."
**Category positioning:** AI travel memory app

---

## Voice & Tone

### The 4 Principles

**1. Warm — this is about memories, not software**
We're helping people preserve moments they care about. Every word should feel human and personal, not technical or corporate.

**2. Confident — the technology speaks for itself**
We don't over-explain the AI or apologize for how it works. We state what Bloggo does clearly and let the result do the talking.

**3. Simple — effortless is the whole point**
If someone has to re-read a sentence to understand it, rewrite it. Short sentences. Plain words. No jargon.

**4. Delightful — small moments of magic matter**
The product surprises people. The copy can too. A little wit goes a long way — but never at the expense of clarity.

---

## Writing Guidelines

### Do
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Use "you" and "your" — make it personal
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
- Active voice always
- Specific > vague ("60 seconds" beats "quickly")

### Don't
- Don't say "leverage," "utilize," "seamless," or "powerful"
- Don't lead with AI/technology — lead with what it does for the user
- Don't use exclamation points more than once per screen
- Don't use passive voice ("blogs are created" → "Bloggo creates")
- Don't mention cloud features — not in V1

### Examples of the right tone

| Wrong | Right |
|-------|-------|
| "Leverage AI to generate narrative content from your photo metadata." | "Bloggo writes the story. You just tap." |
| "Our powerful machine learning algorithms detect trip clusters." | "Bloggo finds your trips automatically — no tagging, no folders." |
| "Seamlessly export your blog as a high-quality PDF." | "Export a beautiful PDF in one tap." |
| "Utilize the Vibe feature to capture ambient audio." | "Capture the sounds of the moment, not just the photo." |

## Core Messaging

### Problem Statement
> "Thousands of photos. Zero stories written."

People take hundreds of photos on every trip and do nothing with them. The memories fade. The photos sit in a camera roll. Bloggo fixes that.

### Solution Statement
> "Bloggo turns your travel photos into beautiful, story-driven blogs in seconds — no writing, no organizing, no blank page."

### The "Why Now" Hook
> "Bloggo removes blank page anxiety."

Most people *want* to document their travels. They just never start. Bloggo removes the starting problem entirely.

### Value Props (ranked by user impact)

1. **Speed** — From photos to shareable blog in under 60 seconds
2. **Zero effort** — AI writes the narratives, detects the trips, organizes the photos
3. **Privacy** — All AI runs on your device. Your photos never leave your phone.
4. **Beautiful output** — PDF, video, interactive story — looks like a professional made it
5. **Unique capture** — Vibe records the ambient sounds of every moment

## Audience Segments

### Primary: The Lapsed Documentarian
- Has a backlog of trips they always meant to write up
- Feels guilty every time they scroll past those photos
- Message: "It's not too late. Bloggo can turn those old trips into blogs right now."

### Secondary: The Active Traveler
- Travels frequently, always takes photos, rarely does more
- Message: "Blog every trip without it taking any extra time."

### Tertiary: The Sharer
- Wants to share trip experiences with friends and family in a polished way
- Message: "Send something beautiful, not just a photo dump."

## Competitive Positioning

**We are NOT:**
- A photo album app (Google Photos, Apple Photos)
- A travel journal app (Day One, Polarsteps)
- A social travel app (BeReal, Instagram)
- A note-taking app with photos

**We ARE:**
- The fastest way to turn travel photos into a real story
- The only app that writes the narrative for you, on your device, privately
- A memory preservation tool that produces shareable, beautiful output

### One-sentence competitive differentiator
> "Unlike other photo or journal apps, Bloggo automatically writes the story of your trip — you don't have to type a single word."
