# Idea Inbox

> Drop raw content ideas here anytime — mid-trip, on the go, or whenever inspiration hits.
> The content strategy workflow checks this file first before generating ideas.
> Your ideas always take priority over AI-generated ideas.

---

## How to Add an Idea

Just write it. No format required. Include as much or as little detail as you want.

Examples:
- "Reel idea — show what happens when you tap to blog for the first time"
- "LinkedIn post — the hardest part of waiting for App Store approval"
- "Carousel — 5 things I wish I knew before building an iOS app"
- "TikTok — I have 3,000 photos from my last trip and zero captions"
