## Content Trend Signals

1. **"Reali-Tea" / Build-in-Public dominates TikTok** — Unfiltered, in-progress content (process videos, creator diaries, work-in-public footage) is outperforming polished brand content. Audiences want honest journeys, not finished products.

2. **"World Stop!" transformation format (Instagram Reels)** — One-take before-and-after Reels are the breakout format for April 2026. Works across beauty, travel, home, food. Directly maps to Bloggo's "chaos → clean blog" story.

3. **Niche over broad — algorithm shift** — Instagram and TikTok now surface niche-specific content in search. Being deeply relevant in travel + builder is worth more than general reach plays. Double down on specificity.

4. **Creator-led authenticity beats brand advertising** — 92% of consumers trust peer recommendations over traditional brand ads. Founders who show up as real people (not brands) build faster, stickier audiences.

5. **Day-in-the-life (30–60 sec) = top travel Reel format** — Short candid travel snippets set to audio with voiceover/subtitles are consistently top performers. Combine with founder narrative for compound reach.

6. **"Build in public" = audience before launch** — Indie hackers who document transparently on X/Instagram launch with 500–5,000 invested followers. Every post is pre-launch marketing.

7. **POV / "this is who" formats on TikTok** — Two-person POV vids and childhood-photo-to-now formats are viral entry points for founder storytelling. Low production, high emotion.

8. **Micro-communities outperform broadcast** — Discord, Substack, close-friends lists are where the actual audience lives in 2026. Building a small group of 100 invested followers beats 10,000 passive ones.
