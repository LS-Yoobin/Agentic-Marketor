## Slide-by-Slide Build Guide

---

### Slide 1 — Hook

**Label:** `AI & WORK` — 14px, gold `#C9A84C`, letter-spaced (tracking: 200+), centered above headline

**Copy:**
> "Companies aren't hiring anymore.
> Here's the data behind why."

**Layout:** Full dark background. Centered. No image.
**Typography:** Label 14px gold. Headline 44px bold white. Line break after "anymore."
**Visual:** None — pure type. The label does the context work.

---

### Slide 2 — The First Hard Stat

**Copy:**
> "41% of employers plan to cut
> headcount using AI."

**Subtext (small, bottom):** `WEF Future of Jobs Report, 2025` — 14px, muted white `#888888`

**Layout:** Centered. Single stat dominates.
**Typography:** `"41%"` — oversized, 80px+, gold. Rest of copy: 32px bold, white.
**Visual:** None — let the number breathe.

---

### Slide 3 — The Scale

**Copy:**
> "92 million jobs
> will be displaced by 2030."

**Layout:** Full slide. One number. Intentionally heavy.
**Typography:** `"92 million"` — oversized, 72px, gold. `"jobs will be displaced by 2030."` — 30px regular, white below.
**Effect:** No subtext. No explanation. The silence is the point.

---

### Slide 4 — The Flip

**Copy:**
> "But 170 million new ones
> will be created."

**Subtext:** `"Net gain: 78 million jobs. The world isn't ending. It's reshaping."`

**Layout:** Two-beat. Big number top. Subtext smaller below, separated by a thin gold line.
**Typography:** `"170 million"` — 72px, gold. `"new ones will be created."` — 32px bold, white. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`.
**Thin gold divider:** `#C9A84C`, 1px, 60% slide width, centered between headline and subtext.

---

### Slide 5 — Who Moves First Wins

**Copy:**
> "Workers with AI skills earn
> 56% more than peers in the same role."

**Subtext:** `"It's not your job title. It's your toolkit."`

**Layout:** Centered. `"56%"` oversized and gold.
**Typography:** `"56%"` — 80px+, gold. Surrounding copy: 28px regular, white. Subtext: 20px italic, muted white.

---

### Slide 6 — Who Survives

**Copy:**
> "It's not the most experienced.
> It's the ones who didn't wait."

**Subtext:** `"They took on more. Built systems around themselves. Same quality. Twice the output. Half the time."`

**Layout:** Left-aligned. Dark background. No image.
**Typography:** Headline 36px bold, white. `"didn't wait."` — in gold. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`, below with breathing room.

---

### Slide 7 — The Uncomfortable Question

**Copy:**
> "But what does that leave
> everyone else?"

**Layout:** Centered. Full `#0D1117`. Nothing else on the slide.
**Typography:** 42px regular, white. Intentionally not bold — the quietness is the tone.
**Effect:** Most sparse slide in the carousel. The pause before the personal answer. Do not add subtext, icons, or images.

---

### Slide 8 — Personal Close

**Copy:**
> "I built a startup with 2 people.
> No big team. No VC. Just the right tools."

> "I understand why companies aren't hiring —
> I lived it from the other side."

**Layout:** Left-aligned. Two separate paragraphs with spacing between.
**Optional visual:** Your photo — circle crop, right column. Same style as previous carousel batch (candid, not formal).
**If no photo:** Full text slide, left-aligned, is equally strong.
**Typography:** 28px regular, white. First paragraph slightly larger (32px) for weight. Second paragraph 26px, muted — lets the honesty land quietly.

---
