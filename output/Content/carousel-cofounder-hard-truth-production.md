## Slide-by-Slide Build Guide

---

### Slide 1 — Hook

**Label:** `CO-FOUNDER TRUTH` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced, centered above headline

**Copy:**
> "Nobody prepares you for this.
> I didn't know what I was signing up for.
> Here's what I know now."

**Layout:** Full dark background. Centered. No image.
**Typography:** Label 14px gold. First line: 38px regular, white. Second line: 38px regular, white. Third line: 32px regular, gold — the turn that earns the swipe.
**Effect:** Three lines, each its own beat. The last line in gold signals "this is personal and honest."

---

### Slide 2 — Truth #1: It All Happens at Once

**Label:** `NOBODY TOLD ME #1`

**Copy:**
> "You're doing things you don't know how to do.
> Making decisions you're not qualified to make.
> Losing sleep over problems with no answers yet.
> All while trying not to lose yourself in the process."

**Subtext:**
> "Nobody tells you it all happens at once."

**Layout:** Left-aligned. Four lines stacked with tight line spacing. Subtext separated by breathing room below, italic.
**Typography:** Body: 28px regular, white. Each line is its own beat — resist combining them. Subtext: 20px italic, gold `#C9A84C`.
**Effect:** The four-line build creates escalating weight. The subtext lands like a gut punch.

---

### Slide 3 — Truth #2: Every Hat

**Label:** `NOBODY TOLD ME #2`

**Copy:**
> "You don't get to choose
> which roles you're good at."

**Subtext (stacked list):**
> Designer. Developer. Marketer.
> Support. Finance. Strategist.
> Some days — all of them before lunch.

**Layout:** Centered headline. List below separated by a thin gold divider line. List left-aligned or centered.
**Typography:** Headline: 36px bold, white. Divider: 1px gold, 50% slide width, centered. List: 22px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`. Last line: 22px italic, white — the twist.

---

### Slide 4 — Truth #3: The Invisible Work

**Label:** `NOBODY TOLD ME #3`

**Copy:**
> "Your peers are getting paid.
> You're getting equity that doesn't exist yet."

**Subtext:**
> "Sweat equity feels like nothing — until it's everything. Feeling behind is part of the deal."

**Layout:** Two-beat headline. First line muted white `#AAAAAA`. Second line bold white — the contrast signals the shift.
**Typography:** First line: 30px regular, muted `#AAAAAA`. Second line: 34px bold, white. Subtext: 20px italic, muted white `#CCCCCC` below with breathing room.
**Effect:** The muted first line makes the second hit harder. Don't make them the same weight.

---

### Slide 5 — Truth #4: Uncertainty Is the Job

**Label:** `NOBODY TOLD ME #4`

**Copy:**
> "There's no roadmap.
> No one telling you if you're on track.
> You have to trust your compass
> when you can't see the horizon."

**Layout:** Centered. Four lines. Generous line spacing.
**Typography:** 32px regular, white. `"trust your compass"` — gold `#C9A84C`. Line break exactly as shown — "when you can't see the horizon." lands on its own line for impact.

---

### Slide 6 — Truth #5: Your Why Is Your Only Fuel

**Label:** `NOBODY TOLD ME #5`

**Copy:**
> "Motivation fades.
> Discipline fades.
> Your why doesn't."

**Subtext:**
> "Write it down. Put it somewhere you'll see it when you want to quit. You will want to quit."

**Layout:** Three short lines centered, oversized. Maximum white space around them.
**Typography:** Each line: 42px bold, white. `"Your why doesn't."` — same size but gold `#C9A84C`. Subtext: 18px italic, muted white below with generous gap.
**Effect:** This is your save-driver slide. The three-line rhythm is screenshot-worthy. Keep it completely uncluttered.

---

### Slide 7 — Truth #6: The Sacrifices Are Real

**Label:** `NOBODY TOLD ME #6`

**Copy:**
> "You don't give things up once.
> You give them up over and over."

**Subtext:**
> "Weekends. Social plans. Comfort. Certainty.
> You accept this — or you don't build.
> There's no middle."

**Layout:** Centered headline. Subtext stacked below with breathing room.
**Typography:** Headline: 36px bold, white. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`. `"There's no middle."` — white, slightly larger (22px), not muted — it's the close of the thought.

---

### Slide 8 — Close (Big Word Format)

**No label. No mascot. No cat paw. Nothing except three elements.**

**Top subtext (small, centered, muted):**
> "After everything —"
> Typography: 20px regular, muted white `#AAAAAA`

**Center hero word (oversized, centered, bold, gold):**
> `WORTH IT.`
> Typography: 96px+ bold, gold `#C9A84C` — as large as it can go with comfortable padding

**Bottom subtext (small, centered, white):**
> "But only you can decide that.
> Follow — I share the journey as it happens."
> Typography: 20px regular, white `#FFFFFF`

**Layout:** Three elements stacked vertically. Equal breathing room above and below the hero word. Slide is perfectly centered — top and bottom subtext act as anchors.
**Background:** Pure `#0D1117` — nothing else. No texture, no mascot, no cat paw.
**Effect:** After 7 slides of hard truths, `WORTH IT.` in gold feels earned. Viewers screenshot this slide. The restraint is intentional — don't add anything.
