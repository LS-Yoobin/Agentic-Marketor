## Slide-by-Slide Build Guide

---

### Slide 1 — Hook

**Label:** `CONTENT CREATION` — 14px, gold `#C9A84C`, letter-spaced (tracking: 200+), centered above headline

**Copy:**
> "I've been creating content
> for over a decade.
> Here's what actually changed."

**Layout:** Full dark background. Centered. No image.
**Typography:** Label 14px gold. Headline 40px bold white. Line break for rhythm — "for over a decade." on its own line.
**Visual:** None — your personal authority is the hook.

---

### Slide 2 — The Old Playbook

**Label:** `THEN` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced, top left

**Copy:**
> "What everyone still thinks works:"

**List:**
- Post more
- More followers = more reach
- Polish beats personality
- Likes are the metric

**Layout:** Left-aligned. Label top. Headline above the list. Each list item as its own line.
**Typography:** Headline 28px regular, muted white `#AAAAAA`. List items 30px bold, white. Small gold dash `—` before each item.
**Effect:** The muted headline signals "this is outdated" before you even read the list.

---

### Slide 3 — What Changed #1 — The Algorithm

**Label:** `WHAT CHANGED — 1 OF 4` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced

**Copy:**
> "The algorithm stopped caring
> about your follower count."

**Subtext:**
> "Saves and watch time decide your reach now — not how many people follow you. A 500-follower account can outperform a 500K one."

**Layout:** Centered. Two-beat: headline bold, subtext smaller below separated by breathing room.
**Typography:** Headline 36px bold, white. `"follower count"` — gold. Subtext 20px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`.

---

### Slide 4 — What Changed #2 — The Format

**Label:** `WHAT CHANGED — 2 OF 4` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced

**Copy:**
> "82% of internet traffic
> is now video."

**Subtext:**
> "A decade ago: blog posts and long YouTube videos. Today: 15 seconds can outperform 15 minutes. Short form isn't a trend. It's the default."

**Layout:** `"82%"` oversized, left-aligned, gold. Rest of headline white below it. Subtext smaller, muted white.
**Typography:** `"82%"` — 80px+, gold. Headline remainder: 32px bold, white. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white.

---

### Slide 5 — What Changed #3 — The Tools

**Label:** `WHAT CHANGED — 3 OF 4` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced

**Copy:**
> "91% of creators now use AI
> in their process."

**Subtext:**
> "What used to take a week of production takes an afternoon. The barrier to entry is gone. The barrier to standing out is higher than ever."

**Layout:** `"91%"` oversized gold. Headline white below. Subtext muted white.
**Typography:** `"91%"` — 80px+, gold. Headline: 32px bold, white. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`.

---

### Slide 6 — What Changed #4 — Monetization

**Label:** `WHAT CHANGED — 4 OF 4` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced

**Copy:**
> "Ad revenue was the end goal.
> Now it's the starting point."

**Subtext:**
> "Subscriptions. Digital products. Communities. The creator economy hit $250B in 2025. The ones winning aren't chasing brand deals — they're building assets."

**Layout:** Two-line headline, centered. Thin gold divider line between headline and subtext.
**Typography:** Headline 34px bold, white. `"starting point."` — gold. Divider: 1px, `#C9A84C`, 60% slide width, centered. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white.

---

### Slide 7 — What Never Changed

**Label:** `WHAT STAYED THE SAME` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced

**Copy:**
> "What a decade didn't change:"

**List:**
- Story
- Consistency
- Knowing why you're creating

**Subtext:**
> "Every platform shifts. Every algorithm updates. These three have never stopped mattering."

**Layout:** Left-aligned. Clean. Generous spacing between list items.
**Typography:** Headline 26px regular, muted white. List items 38px bold, white — larger than anything else on the slide. Subtext 18px italic, muted white below.
**Effect:** This is your save-driver slide. The oversized list items make it screenshot-worthy. Keep it uncluttered — resist adding icons or decorations.

---

### Slide 8 — How to Approach It Now

**Copy:**
> "Stop trying to post more.
> Start making content
> worth saving."

**Subtext:**
> "One piece that teaches, challenges, or resonates will always beat ten that disappear into the feed."

**Layout:** Centered. Punchy. Nothing else.
**Typography:** `"Stop trying to post more."` — 30px regular, muted white `#AAAAAA`. `"Start making content worth saving."` — 38px bold, white. `"worth saving"` — gold. Subtext: 18px italic, muted white below.
**Effect:** The contrast between the muted first line and bold second line creates the visual emphasis without needing design tricks.

---

## Canva Build Order

1. Start with `#0D1117` background template, 1080x1080
2. Build Slide 9 (CTA) first — base template for all slides
3. Build Slides 3, 4, 5, 6 — the "What Changed" slides (numbered series, consistent label style)
4. Build Slide 7 — the save-driver, keep it spacious
5. Build Slide 2 (Old Playbook) — muted headline signals the outdated framing
6. Build Slide 8 (How to approach now) — the action slide
7. Build Slide 1 (Hook) last — tweak headline once you see the full arc
8. Export all as PNG, 300dpi
