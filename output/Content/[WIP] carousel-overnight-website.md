## Design Notes

- **Color palette:** Dark background, white text — consistent with your Yoobin portfolio feel
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif for headlines, lighter weight for bullets
- **Slide ratio:** 1080 x 1350px (portrait) — max real estate in feed
- **Slide 1 must do the heavy lifting** — if it doesn't stop the scroll, nothing else matters
- **Slides 4 + 5 are the value core** — don't rush the Cursor and Claude copy, these are what people save
- **Slide 9 is the emotional payoff** — screenshot of the live site goes here, not earlier
