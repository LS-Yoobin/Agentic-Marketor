# Talking Head Reel — Data Ownership & Longevity

**Posted from:** Bloggo account (@bloggo)
**Notion destination:** notion → Bloggo Content Engine
**Platform:** TikTok / Instagram Reels
**Format:** Talking head, direct to camera
**Target duration:** 45–60 seconds
**Context:** Response to viral comment concern — "what if you just shut down one day and everyone loses their data"

---

## Hook (0–3 sec)

**On-screen text:** "What happens to your blog if we shut down?"

**Speak:**
> "Someone asked us a really fair question — what if Bloggo shuts down one day? Do you just lose everything?"

---

## Body (3–45 sec)

**Speak:**
> "So here's the honest answer — and this is actually why we built Bloggo the way we did.

> Your blogs don't live on our servers. They never did. Bloggo is what's called local-first blogging, which means everything happens on your phone. Your photos never leave your device. The AI that reads your camera roll and writes your travel story? That's running on your iPhone — not on our machines, not in our cloud.

> So if Bloggo disappeared tomorrow — and I'm not planning on that — your blogs would still be sitting right there on your phone. We can't take what we never held.

> And on top of that, we give you five ways to take your stories anywhere. You can export a PDF storybook, create social media slides for Instagram or TikTok, generate a slideshow video, send a share link, or transfer it directly to another device with no internet at all.

> We built it this way because we know how much your memories matter. They're not supposed to have a middleman."

---

## CTA (45–60 sec)

**Speak:**
> "So — your stories are yours. That was always the point. Download Bloggo, link in bio."

**On-screen text:** "Your stories. Your phone. Forever. 🔗 Link in bio"

## Production Notes

- Speak directly to camera. Conversational, not scripted-sounding.
- Keep pace confident but warm — this is reassurance, not a pitch.
- No need for cuts or b-roll. A clean, direct response works best here.
