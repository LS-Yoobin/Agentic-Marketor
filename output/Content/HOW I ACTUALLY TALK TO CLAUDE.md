## Design Rules (apply to every slide)

- **Max 8 lines of text per slide** — if it's more, cut it
- **One idea per slide** — never two
- **Analogy always closes the tip slides** — it's what makes it stick
- **Slide 6 is the most actionable slide** — make "CLAUDE.md" visually prominent, exact spelling matters
- **Slide 11 is viewer-facing** — "you" not "I", feels like a promise
- **Slide 1 and Slide 12 are the most important** — hook and CTA carry the carousel
- **1080 x 1350px portrait** — max feed real estate
- **Dark background, white type** — consistent with carousel 1 and your Yoobin portfolio
