# Bloggo Video Series: "This Trip Deserved a Story"
**Date:** April 15, 2026
**Account:** Bloggo brand (@bloggoapp)
**Platforms:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
**Format:** 7–12 seconds | B-roll → iPhone mockup reveal → CTA

---

## Series Concept

A destination-per-video series. Each video asks the same implicit question — *why are your travel photos just sitting there?* — and answers it visually in under 12 seconds. Infinitely scalable: any destination = a new video.

---

## Video Structure (Every Video)

| Timing | What Happens |
|--------|--------------|
| 0–2s | B-roll plays. Hook text appears on screen. |
| 2–4s | Transition (zoom in to iPhone mockup) |
| 4–9s | iPhone mockup animates — Bloggo blog from that place |
| 9–12s | CTA card: "Turn your trips into blogs. Download Bloggo." |

---

## Hook Formulas (Rotate These)

1. **Self-identification:** `"If you're watching this, you have a [destination] trip that's still just photos."`
2. **Curiosity gap:** `"Nobody told you your camera roll could do this."`
3. **Nostalgia/guilt:** `"That trip you took last year? It's already fading."`
4. **Reframe:** `"A camera roll is not a memory. A story is."`
5. **Direct address:** `"If you're watching this and you haven't blogged that trip — this is your sign."`

---

## Psychology Behind the Hooks

- **Self-identification** — Brain flags content that feels like it's *about me*
- **Curiosity gap** — Open a loop without closing it. The brain needs to resolve it.
- **Emotional memory** — People remember feelings more than facts. Lead with nostalgia or guilt, not features.
- **65% rule** — Viewers who stay past 3 seconds watch for 10+. The hook has 3 seconds.

---

## Audio Strategy

| Audio Type | When to Use | Effect |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| Soft piano build | Nostalgia hooks | Triggers emotional memory |
| Cinematic swell | Aspirational destinations | Peaks on mockup reveal |
| Trending audio | All videos | Algorithm boost |
| Silence → sound drop | High-drama destinations (Iceland) | Maximum pattern interrupt |
| Ambient destination sound | Layered under music at 20% | Immersion and authenticity |

**Rule:** Time the music drop or build peak to land on the exact frame the iPhone mockup appears.
