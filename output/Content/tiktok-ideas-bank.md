## Format Reference

| Format | Best for | Avg production time |
|--------|----------|---------------------|
| 10-slide | Deep dives, emotional lists, problem arcs | 90 min |
| 5-slide | Feature reveals, comparisons, short lists | 45 min |
| 1-slide | Statements, quotes, memes, comment bait | 10 min |

## Audio Reference

| Sound | Use for | Status |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Love Between the Lines | Emotional, nostalgia, problem posts | Proven ✓ |
| Trending melancholic acoustic | Guilt, loss, quiet posts | Search weekly |
| Trending upbeat/satisfying | Feature demos, product posts | Search weekly |
| Trending relatable/humor | Meme format, POV posts | Search weekly |

**How to find trending audio:** TikTok search → Sounds tab → filter "This Week" → pick 50k–500k posts (enough reach, not oversaturated)
