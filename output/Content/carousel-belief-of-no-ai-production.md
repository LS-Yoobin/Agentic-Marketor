## Slide-by-Slide Build Guide

---

### Slide 1 — Hook

**Label:** `MY TAKE ON AI` — 14px, gold `#C9A84C`, letter-spaced, centered above headline

**Copy:**
> "I fixed my own car.
> Built a mobile app in 2 months.
> Launched multiple websites.
> I had zero experience in any of them."

**Subtext:**
> "AI made all of it possible. Here's how I actually think about it."

**Layout:** Left-aligned. Four lines stacked, each its own beat. Subtext italic, muted white `#CCCCCC` below with breathing room.
**Typography:** List lines: 32px regular, white. Subtext: 18px italic, muted white.
**Effect:** The specificity of these three things is the hook. Don't summarize — let all four lines breathe.

---

### Slide 2 — Proof #1: The Car

**Label:** `PROOF` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced, top center

**Copy:**
> "I repaired my own car.
> No mechanical background.
> No idea where to start."

**Subtext:**
> "I described the problem. AI walked me through every step. I did the work. It guided the uncertainty."

**Layout:** Centered. Three punchy lines. Subtext muted white below.
**Typography:** Lines: 36px bold, white. Subtext: 20px regular, muted white `#CCCCCC`.
**Effect:** Short lines read fast. The subtext explains the method without over-explaining.

---

### Slide 3 — Proof #2: The App

**Label:** `PROOF` — 14px, gold, letter-spaced, top center

**Copy:**
> "I built a mobile app in 2 months.
> No CS degree.
> Just the right questions — asked the right way."

**Subtext:**
> "That app is now waiting for App Store approval."

**Typography:** Lines: 34px bold, white. `"2 months"` — gold. Subtext: 18px italic, muted white.
**Effect:** The subtext adds real credibility — it's not hypothetical, it's happening now.

### Slide 4 — The Bridge

**Copy:**
> "None of these were in my wheelhouse.
> All of them felt impossible before I started."

**Subtext:**
> "The common thread wasn't talent. It was having an assistant for every moment of uncertainty."

**Layout:** Two-beat headline. Thin gold divider between headline and subtext.
**Typography:** First line: 30px regular, muted white `#AAAAAA`. Second line: 34px bold, white. Divider: 1px gold, 50% slide width, centered. Subtext: 20px regular, gold `#C9A84C`.
**Effect:** Muted first line → bold second line creates escalating weight. Subtext in gold signals this is the key idea.

### Slide 5 — The Framework

**Copy:**
> "Approach AI as an assistant
> that is here for every aspect of your life
> when it comes to uncertainty."

**Subtext:**
> "Not a replacement. Not a shortcut. A guide through the unknown."

**Layout:** Centered. Three lines. Maximum breathing room.
**Typography:** Lines: 32px regular, white. `"uncertainty"` — gold `#C9A84C`. Subtext: 18px italic, muted white below with generous gap.
**Effect:** This is the thesis of the carousel. Keep the slide clean — it should feel like a principle, not a paragraph.
